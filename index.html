About简介

My research focuses on combustion and explosion phenomena in energy systems, with recent work spanning lithium-ion battery vent gases, hydrogen safety, droplet and spray effects, autoignition, and kinetic modeling for practical fuels. 我的研究聚焦能源系统中的燃烧与爆炸现象，近期工作涉及锂离子电池泄放气体、氢能安全、液滴与喷雾效应、自燃以及实际燃料化学动力学建模等方向。
